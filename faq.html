Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a coaching session?

You can book directly through the “Book Now” button on our homepage. It will open a form where you can select your game

What games do you coach?

Currently, we offer coaching for Valorant and Mobile Legends. More titles may be added in the future based on demand.

How are sessions conducted?

Sessions are held online via Discord. We use screen share, replay reviews, and live gameplay to guide you step by step.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We run seasonal promotions (like Halloween or New Year discounts). Check the pricing section on our homepage for current offers.

Can I reschedule a session?

Absolutely. We understand schedules can change. Just let us know at least 24 hours in advance, and we’ll adjust your booking.
